Frequently Asked Questions

When am I most fertile?

You are most fertile during your fertile window, which spans approximately 5 days before ovulation and the day of ovulation itself. For a 28-day cycle, this is typically days 10-16.

How do I calculate my ovulation date?

Ovulation typically occurs 14 days before your next period. Enter the first day of your last period and your average cycle length into our calculator for a personalized prediction.

How long does ovulation last?

The egg is viable for 12-24 hours after ovulation. However, sperm can survive 3-5 days in the reproductive tract, making the fertile window several days wide.

Can ovulation dates vary each month?

Yes — stress, illness, weight changes, and other factors can shift ovulation timing. This calculator provides an estimate based on average cycle patterns.

Is this calculator suitable for birth control?

No. This tool is for informational purposes only and is not reliable for contraception. Please consult a healthcare provider for birth control guidance.
